vegetarian food

Awareness of eating vegetarian food because it is the     growth of the body in a healthy vegetarian system tha      preserves diseases and the growth of the environment      and animals.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil: 3 tablespoons
  • Garlic: cloves
  • Eggplant: 1 piece
  • Tomato: can
  • Macaroni: a package
  1. Heat the olive oil in a wide, deep saucepan over a medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and stir for two minutes.
  3. Add the eggplant and cook for 5 minutes until softened, then add the tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Boil the pasta and drain it, add it to the eggplant mixture, add the olives and leave it on the fire for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour the pasta into a serving dish and serve hot.
